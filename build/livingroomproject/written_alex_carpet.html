ALEX'S

story about his guitar
I bought an ethnic carpet in Uyuni, near the salt desert in Bolivia. This one reminds me of it.

At the time we were driving a jeep
through the desert when I noticed our driver dozing off. A week before that, a group of travellers had a fatal accident
at the same place.

I slapped our driver to wake him up.
