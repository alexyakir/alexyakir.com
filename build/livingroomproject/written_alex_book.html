Life a user's manual, by George Perec, presents an entire block, frozen in time. In that block are hundreds of separate stories concerning the inhabitants of the block and other people in their lives.

I bought this book on my first day at the army service
as a present for my mom. She couldn't finish it so I started reading it, which took me the entire 3 years of my army service.
